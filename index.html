Biggest Takeaways

When I was going through my Arduino Project there were several big takeaways that I'm sure will dictate my everlasting success as an engineer. One, it is very important to fully dive into a subject that will fit your needs and what you want to do in life. Initially, the project seemed easy to me, I was going to type a few lines of code, learn what an arduino does, and how a circut board works. These things seemed so simple to me at the time, but boy was I in for a whole new world. I started to dive into the world of circuts, C++, CSS, Java-to-HTML/C++ conversion documents, configuring libraries in proportion to electronic setup, pin-declaration, and much much more. Through this experience I learned that I want to dive into the world of electronics and software.

I'm very appreciatiative of this experience and I do hope I can have more connections in the future.
